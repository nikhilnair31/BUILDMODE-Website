PRIVACY POLICY

WE DON'T TRACK. WE DON'T ORGANIZE. WE DON'T ASK QUESTIONS.
FORGOR SAVES WHAT YOU ASK, AND FORGETS EVERYTHING ELSE.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • SAVED CONTENT: IMAGES, TEXT, LINKS, AND SCREENSHOTS YOU CHOOSE TO SAVE.
  • ACCOUNT INFO: WE STORE YOUR EMAIL FOR LOGIN, SYNCING, AND OPTIONAL DIGEST EMAILS.
  • BASIC USAGE LOGS: NON-PERSONAL CRASH OR PERFORMANCE DATA, USED TO IMPROVE THE APP.

2. HOW WE USE IT

  • TO SAVE AND RETRIEVE YOUR CONTENT WITH ACCURATE AI TAGGING.
  • TO OFFER SMART SUGGESTIONS WHILE TYPING VIA THE CHROME EXTENSION.
  • TO SEND OPTIONAL SUMMARY EMAILS (WEEKLY/MONTHLY, IF ENABLED).
  • TO MAINTAIN APP RELIABILITY AND FIX BUGS.

3. WHAT WE NEVER DO

  • NO SELLING YOUR DATA.
  • NO ADS OR THIRD-PARTY ANALYTICS.
  • NO CROSS-SITE TRACKING.

4. YOUR CONTROL

  • YOU CAN DELETE SAVED CONTENT AT ANY TIME.
  • YOU CAN REQUEST FULL ACCOUNT DELETION VIA EMAIL.
  • YOU CAN OPT OUT OF DIGEST EMAILS.

5. SECURITY

YOUR DATA IS ENCRYPTED IN TRANSIT. ACCOUNT ACCESS IS PROTECTED WITH TOKEN-BASED AUTHENTICATION.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

WE MAY USE SECURE CLOUD PROVIDERS (E.G. FIREBASE, AWS) TO STORE YOUR DATA. THESE SERVICES COMPLY WITH INDUSTRY SECURITY STANDARDS.

7. CHILDREN

FORGOR IS NOT INTENDED FOR CHILDREN UNDER 13. WE DO NOT KNOWINGLY COLLECT DATA FROM CHILDREN.
